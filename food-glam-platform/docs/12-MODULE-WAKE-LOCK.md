# Module: Wake Lock (Prevent Standby)## Goal Attempt to prevent phone standby while: -viewing a recipe (optional toggle "Keep screen on") -watching a video in popup (auto while modal open/play) ## Tech-Screen Wake Lock API: `navigator.wakeLock.request('screen')`## Constraints-Not guaranteed on all browsers (notably iOS Safari). -Must degrade gracefully. ## Acceptance criteria-On supported browsers, screen stays awake during cooking/video. -On unsupported, show non-blocking info.
